General Discussion Questions

PCF Metrics is very much in its early stages (it is still labeled a beta). So don’t oversell its capabilities.

It is known that right now it doesn’t help you pinpoint exactly where the problem is but further iterations (release of the product) will be moving towards more integrated views, such as including logs for a specific point in time and support for individual instances (at present everything is aggregated/averaged).
